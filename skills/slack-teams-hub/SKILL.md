# Slack/Teams Notification Hub

## When to Use
- Daily briefing delivery to Slack
- Urgent deal or system alerts
- Competitive news notifications
- Cross-team status updates

## Capabilities (Planned)
- Post formatted messages to Slack channels
- Monitor channels for @mentions or keywords
- Send daily briefing digest to designated channel
- Alert on earnings releases or competitor news
- Integrate with Salesforce deal alerts

## Prerequisites
- Slack Bot token (via 1Password Jarvis account)
- Teams webhook URL (optional)
- Channel permissions configured by Cohesity IT
