## Idea #1: AI-Powered M&A & Deal Sourcing Intelligence Feed

### 1. Business Concept
Jarvis continuously monitors hundreds of private and public companies for M&A signals: executive departures on LinkedIn, layoff notices, regulatory filings, VC funding gaps, job posting patterns, and competitive press releases. The output is a curated, weekly intelligence brief sold to PE/VC firms and M&A advisors who need early signals before deals get shopped.

This is the "Bloomberg for private company distress signals" — and it doesn't require a Bloomberg-sized team because Jarvis runs the scraping, analysis, and synthesis pipeline. Eric's Cohesity CFO/COO role gives him instant credibility with the exact buyers (PE portcos, growth equity investors) who will pay serious money for an edge.

### 2. Target Market & TAM
- **Primary buyers:** PE firms (6,000+ in the US), VC firms, M&A boutique advisors, corporate development teams
- **Alternative data market TAM:** ~$10B globally by 2026 (Mordor Intelligence)
- **Serviceable segment:** 500–2,000 firms willing to pay $1K–$10K/month for proprietary deal signals
- **SAM estimate:** ~$500M–$1B

### 3. Revenue Model & Pricing
| Tier | Price/Month | Inclusions |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Signal | $500/mo | Weekly digest, 1 sector focus |
| Intelligence | $2,000/mo | Daily alerts, 3 sectors, custom watchlists |
| Analyst | $5,000/mo | Real-time feed, API access, bespoke reports |

Revenue model: **SaaS subscription** with 12-month contracts and annual prepay discount (10%).

### 4. What Jarvis Does Autonomously vs. What Needs Eric

**Jarvis (autonomous):**
- ProjectScraper runs 24/7 on LinkedIn, SEC EDGAR, job boards, press release wires, Crunchbase, court filings
- OpenClaw agents analyze signal patterns and score deal probability
- Auto-generate formatted intelligence briefs in Markdown/PDF
- Monitor customer watchlists and send real-time Telegram/email alerts
- Track accuracy and refine signal models

**Eric's contribution (2–4 hrs/week):**
- Initial credibility: "from the CFO of Cohesity" as brand anchor
- Warm intros to first 10–20 PE/VC customers (existing network)
- Validate signal quality on early deals (sanity check)
- Negotiate enterprise contracts
- Hire one analyst (optional) once revenue > $20K/mo

Eric writes the editorial thesis (1–2 hours per report). Jarvis does all the data collection, analysis, charting, and first-draft writing. Output: 30–60 page institutional-quality PDF report with proprietary analysis not available elsewhere.

### 2. Target Market & TAM
- **Financial research market:** ~$7B globally (Bloomberg Intelligence, Gartner, Forrester, IDC)
